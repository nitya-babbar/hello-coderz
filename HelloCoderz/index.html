What is Hello Coderz?

Hello Coderz is a platform that encourages young students to learn the core of programming in a fun and engaging way. There are two main parts to Hello Coderz: lessons and contests.


Lessons
Contests

Hello Coderz lessons are minimally priced with qualified instructors. To keep them engaged, students learn at a pace that is personalized to them. Students sign up for one session (consisting of 10 one-hour lessons) at a time. Lessons can be private, in groups of two, or in groups of three (prices vary). All classes are completed virtually through Zoom, and students receive about an hour of homework to complete by their next class. Currently, Hello Coderz teaches Python; Java and C++ curriculum are being constructed.

Hello Coderz offers free programming contests to middle and high school students from any location, worldwide. They take place on the first Saturday of each month. Teams of up to three students can participate. There is a different set of problems for each division: middle school and high school. A prize will be given to the first place team in each division. These contests are a great way for new programmers to test the waters! For more information, and to register for the contests, visit the Contests page.

Philosophy

We believe that with the ability to code, students can dream bigger and achieve more. Our philosophy is to not only teach children how to code, but to show our students the joy in coding. Like a rising balloon, if students enjoy what they are doing, they can rise up to any challenge. Students learn best from those that are closer to their age, and through their high school / college instructors, they will not only learn to program but will also have a role model to look up to.

Learning to code not only develops programming skills, but also logic and analytical thinking skills that can be used everywhere. We believe that all students deserve a chance to explore the benefits of coding regardless of financial backgrounds. This is why Hello Coderz programs are significantly lower in cost as compared to the rest of the market. Our goal is to introduce more students from all economic backgrounds to the tech world at a younger age, help them grow confidence in coding, and ultimately encourage them to pursue further education in a STEM field.
